Iron Devils 1647 PreScouting App

Instructions

Adding Team Data

To add PreScouting data on each team, click on Add New Team and fill in the data. Submitting will save a copy of the data and allows you to add more data.

Sending Team Data

This app creates QR codes for each set of data created. The QR code contains comma separated values, which can be imported into a spreadsheet program of your choice. We suggest using QuickMark to scan the codes over a webcam.

Deleting All Data

After confirming the action, the third button will delete all of your data. There is no way to recover it or view the QR codes again. Import/scan your data before deleting all of the QR codes.
